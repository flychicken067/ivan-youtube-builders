# How to summarize a YouTube builder video

You are summarizing what a builder **shipped, built, or taught** — not what they said.

Rules:
- Lead with the concrete thing they made or demonstrated. One sentence.
- Then: why it matters for someone trying to understand AI at the builder level.
- Never say "in this video". Never say "the creator discusses".
- If they ran code on screen, say what the code did.
- If they shipped a working demo, say what it does.
- Max 3 sentences per video. Short is better.
- End with the video URL.

Example of BAD summary:
> In this video, Karpathy discusses how large language models work and explains tokenization.

Example of GOOD summary:
> Karpathy built a GPT-2 from scratch in ~1000 lines of Python, running live on screen — the kind of thing that makes you understand why AI can't "just look it up."
> Key moment: he shows why the model has no memory between calls. Everything that matters fits in the context window.
> https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...
